Frequently Asked Questions

Are these tools really free to use?

Yes, absolutely! All tools on ImageSolutions are completely free to use without any limitations on the number of images you can process. We believe everyone should have access to high-quality image utilities.

How is my privacy and data protected?

Your privacy is our top priority. Our tools work entirely within your web browser. This means your images are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party service. They remain on your device at all times.

What image file types are supported?

We support all the most common image formats, including JPG/JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP, and BMP. Our converter tool can help you switch between formats like JPG, PNG, and WebP with ease.

Do I need to install any software?

No software installation is required. ImageSolutions is a fully web-based application. As long as you have a modern web browser (like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge), you can use all of our tools instantly.
